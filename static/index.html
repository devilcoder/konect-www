The KONECT Project

Jérôme Kunegis
University of Namur

This is the KONECT project, an academic project in the area of network science with the goal to collect network datasets, analyse them, and make available all analyses online. KONECT is run by Jérôme Kunegis at the University of Namur, at the Namur Center for Complex Networks.

For a general overview, see the KONECT Handbook.
